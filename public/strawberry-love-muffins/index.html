Strawberry Love Muffins

madi/hamid

Jan 1, 2024 baking breakfast muffins

Servings

12 Servings

Prep time

--- Minutes

Cooling Time

--- Minutes

Calories

--- Kcal

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened plant milk
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F / 190°C
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Mix wet ingredients separately then fold into dry
  4. Fold in strawberries
  5. Fill muffin tin 3/4 full
  6. Bake 20-25 minutes until golden
