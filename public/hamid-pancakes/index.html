Original recipe

  • I have 3 scoops, 1 large “half cup”, 1 medium “2 tbsp” and 1 small “half tsp”

  • dry: 1.5 cups ww flour, .5 tsp baking soda, 1.5 tsp baking powder, .25 tsp salt

  • wet: 2 tbsp flax meal mixed with 6 tbsp water + 1.5 cups soy milk mixed with 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar and 2 tbsp maple syrup or molasses + one mashed banana

  1. Mix dry, mix flax egg, mix milk, mix egg and milk, mash the banana and add some of the milk to it to loosen, add the wet into the flour and stir, add the banana and fold in

  2. cook on 350 f griddle 1/4 cup sized dollops

Notes on “scoops”

the 2 tbsp scoop is a coffee scoop, it helps bc the egg is 1 medium scoop flax + 3 medium scoops water, the milk is 3 large scoops + 1 medium scoop vinegar + 1 medium scoop maple syrup

the dry is 3 large scoops of flour, 1 small scoop of baking soda, 3 small scoop of baking powder, dash salt

Converted Recipe

  1. 250 g whole wheat flour (350 ml/1.5 cups)

  2. 2 g baking soda (2.5 ml/ .5 tsp)

  3. 6 g baking powder (7.5 ml / 1.5 tsp)

  4. 2 g salt (1.25 ml/ .25 tsp )

  5. 350 g soy milk (350 ml/1.5 cups)

  6. 40 g molassess (30 ml/2 tbsp)

  7. 30 g apple cider vinegar (30ml/2tbsp)

  8. 15 g flax meal

  9. 85g water (85 ml water/6 tbsp)

  • Mix dry ingredients

  • Mix wet ingredients

  • Mix flax egg to thicken

  • Mix flax egg with wet ingredients

  • Mix wet and dry

  • Cook on griddle at 175c/350 f
