Oatmeal raisin cookies

- 1 cups almonds (roasted, blended)
- 1/4 cup flax egg (1 tablespoon Flax meal to 1/4 water)
- 1/2 cup date syrup (6 Dates up to 1/2 cup with water)
- 1 teaspoon baking soda, cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup soy flour (roasted)
- 1 cup oats
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 350 for 10-12 minutes

I often add random stuff I'm trying to get rid of into the batter or mixins, and it's very light on sugar so feel free to double that and/or not use date syrup (maple syrup or sugar with a little water works fine). You can also get away with any binding flour, as long as it's not just oats. And if you can scoop it and it sticks together, it will cookie. Very flexible!
