# Ingredients:
- 150 ml soy milk
- 3 tbsp tapioca starch
- 40 ml olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp lemon juice

# Procedure:

Whisk together, or shake in a bottle/jar, and pour on top of pizza, on quesadillas, etc. The starch activates with heat so you need to heat it up in some way!

# Recommendations:

Not in the original recipe but I've tried different versions of the base recipe, adding nooch and artificial vegan "blue cheese" flavouring, lactic acid instead of lemon juice, neutral oil, msg... It's pretty versatile, I suggest trying different things 🙏
