This foolproof, oil-free whole wheat loaf uses a small amount of unsweetened applesauce to maintain moisture and a soft crumb without adding any apple flavor. Blending whole wheat with all-purpose flour ensures a fantastic, high rise every time.

* **Prep time:** 10 minutes
* **Bake time:** Active machine cycle (~3 hours 40 minutes)
* **Yield:** 1 Loaf (1.5 lb size)

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Water (lukewarm)** | 1 ¼ cups (300 ml) | Target roughly 80°F–90°F |
| **Unsweetened Applesauce** | 2 tbsp | Natural oil replacement for moisture |
| **Sugar or Maple Syrup** | 1 ½ tbsp | Feeds the yeast and aids browning |
| **Salt** | 1 ½ tsp | Regulates the rise and enhances flavor |
| **Whole Wheat Flour** | 2 cups | Rich, nutty whole grain base |
| **All-Purpose Flour** | 1 cup | Provides structural gluten support |
| **Instant / Bread Machine Yeast** | 2 tsp | Keep dry until the cycle starts |

## Instructions

1. **Layer the Liquids:** Pour the lukewarm water, unsweetened applesauce, and sugar (or maple syrup) directly into the bottom of your bread machine pan.
2. **Layer the Dry Ingredients:** Gently add the whole wheat flour and all-purpose flour over the liquid, leveling it out so it completely blankets the wet ingredients. Sprinkle the salt around the outer edges of the flour.
3. **Create a Yeast Well:** Dig a small, shallow indentation (a "well") in the center of the flour mound. Ensure it does not go deep enough to touch the water below. Pour the instant yeast directly into the well.
4. **Machine Settings:** Program your machine for a **1.5 lb Loaf**, select the **Wheat** cycle (to allow for proper hydration and rising time), and set the crust control to **Medium**. Start the machine.
5. **Check the Dough Ball:** About 5 to 10 minutes into the first kneading cycle, open the lid to check the consistency. The dough should form a smooth, tacky ball that cleanly clears the sides of the pan. If it is too wet, add 1 tablespoon of flour. If too dry, add 1 teaspoon of water.
6. **Cool and Slice:** Once baking is complete, remove the pan immediately. Let the bread rest in the pan for 5 minutes, then invert it onto a wire rack. Allow the loaf to cool completely for at least 30 to 45 minutes before slicing to preserve its moisture.
