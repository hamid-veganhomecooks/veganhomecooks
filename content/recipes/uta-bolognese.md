**INGREDIENTS :**

- 2 medium onions
- 1 carrot
- 2 celery sticks
- 2 cloves of garlic
- Tomato paste
- 100 grams TVP
- 500 grams tomato passata
- Soy sauce
- Veggie broth
- Paprika
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Italian seasoning mix
- Red wine (Alternatively White wine, or vegetable vinegar)

**Step 1**

Soak the tvp in a mixture of veggie broth with a splash of soy sauce for 20 minutes.
After 20 minutes, drain the tvp in a sieve.


**STEP 2**

Dice the onions, then start cooking them in a bit of olive oil on medium heat until translucent. While they are cooking dice the celery sticks and grate the carrot finely.

**Step 3**

Add the celery, carrots and tvp to the pan, then add a bit of olive oil and put on high heat. keep cooking it until lightly browned.

**Step 4**

Cut the garlic finely, or crush it and add it along with the tomato paste in a bit of olive oil on the same pan. Cook it for just a bit.

**Step 5**

Add a good splash of red wine or alternative and then reduce the heat to low. Deglaze the pan with the wine.

**Step 6**

Add 500 grams of tomato passata, then add salt, pepper and paprika to taste. Now cook for 1 hour covered. You only need to stir every 15 minutes or so.

**Step 7** 

Add a bit of the italian seasoning mix at the end and you are finished! Feel free to add vegan grated cheese or nutritional yeast.
