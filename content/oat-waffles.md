## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup water
- Salt to taste
- Optional: baking powder, date sugar, regular sugar, cinnamon, or other spices to taste
 
## Directions
 
1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
2. Blend together into a smooth batter.
3. Pour into a waffle iron and cook according to your waffle iron's instructions.
 
## Notes
 
- No need to make oat flour first — blending the oats and water together all at once gives the best results.
- Use the optional add-ins to customize the flavor to your liking.
