> Yields a single crust. Double the recipe to make a top and bottom crust.
 
## Ingredients
 
- 1 1/2 cups ground oats or oat flour
- 3 tbsp tahini or other seed butter
- 1/4 cup date paste (made by blending 6 medjool dates + 1/3 cup water — this will make more than needed; store the extra in the fridge)
- 1/2 cup warm water (adjust as needed)
- Salt to taste (optional)
 
## Directions
 
1. Mix the date paste and tahini together until well combined.
2. Add the mixture to the oat flour and stir to incorporate.
3. Slowly add warm water while mixing and kneading until a smooth, playdough-like consistency is achieved.
4. Place the dough ball between two pieces of parchment paper and roll out into a circle large enough to cover a 9-inch pan.
5. Trim the edges, add your filling, and add a top crust if desired.
 
## Notes
 
- To prevent the dough from drying out while working, cover it with a damp cloth.
- If the dough becomes dry, add a little more water and knead it in well.
- Adjust baking temperature depending on the type of pie you are making.
- Bake covered for the first half of the bake time to prevent the edges from burning, then uncover for the remainder.
