The Formula

- 14 oz can no-salt beans
- half cup rolled oats
- half cup cooked brown rice (or any starch)
- 2 tbsp tomato product (tomato sauce, paste, crushed tomatoes etc)
- 2 tbsp mixed spice (be creative!)

mash them all up and bake!
